Hungry Duck

By Nikki and Ivy

In Hungry Duck, the objective is to feed the duck until it is fat and happy. There is bread spread out around the pond--when you see a piece, go get it!

Instructions:

  • Use the arrows on the keyboard to move the duck left, right, up, or down.
  • Navigate the duck to pieces of bread. There will be a variety of bread products.
  • As the duck eats more, he will get bigger.
